# Agent instructions — Skills-Architect-UK



## Professional skill activation



When the user's request involves **UK architecture, planning, building regulations, construction delivery, or practice operations**, read and follow:



`uk-architect-master/SKILL.md` (`uk-architect-master`)



Include:



- Sub-skill routing from `uk-architect-master/skills.json` or Section 5 of `SKILL.md`

- Compliance halt rules in `uk-architect-master/references/compliance.md`

- Intake checklist in `uk-architect-master/references/operational.md`

- Domain terms in `uk-architect-master/references/domain_terms.json`



Dispatch to `uk-architect-master/subskills/<slug>/<slug>.md` for the selected module.



Use `load_sub_skill` via `uk-architect-master/main.py` when deep module instructions are required.



Use `run_uk_calculator` for advisory Part L/O, IPMS, and egress screens only — not as statutory sign-off.



Do not provide formal legal advice, structural certification, or planning determinations.
