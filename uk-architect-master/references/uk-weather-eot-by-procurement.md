# UK weather and delay — treatment by procurement route

Exceptionally adverse weather and delay events under common UK contract forms. Legal interpretation → counsel.

## JCT 2024 (Standard / Intermediate / D&B)

| Event | Treatment | Notice |
|-------|-----------|--------|
| Exceptionally adverse weather | Relevant event (not always compensable — check contract) | Delay notice per contract; contemporary records |
| Force majeure | Contract-specific; check amendment | As contract |
| Statutory holiday | Usually excluded from EOT | — |

**Architect/CA role:** Assess whether event qualifies; request contemporary weather records (Met Office); do not determine without contract grounds.

## NEC4 ECC

| Event | Treatment | Notice |
|-------|-----------|--------|
| Weather measurement | Compensation event if exceeds 1-in-10 year values at weather station | Early warning + compensation event notification |
| Force majeure | Clause 19 / secondary options | Early warning |

**Project Manager role:** Assess CE; time and cost per contract mechanism.

## Management contracting

| Event | Treatment |
|-------|-----------|
| Weather | Trade package level; manager coordinates |
| Interface delay | Programme float and trade coordination |

## Record-keeping (all routes)

- [ ] Daily site records with weather conditions
- [ ] Met Office or site station data for claimed periods
- [ ] Programme impact analysis (as-planned vs as-built)
- [ ] Notice issued within contract period
