# UK site establishment — pre-construction checklist

Gate before main works commence. Cross-check `uk-consent-scheduling` for BC approval status.

## Consent and approvals

- [ ] Planning permission and discharged conditions (where material to start)
- [ ] Building Control Full Plans approval / commencement conditions met
- [ ] HRB Gateway 2 approval (if HRB)
- [ ] Party Wall notices served; awards in place where required
- [ ] Hoarding permit approved (local authority / TfL)
- [ ] Chapter 8 traffic management approved (highways / TfL)

## Utilities and telecom

- [ ] DIAN / utility records obtained
- [ ] Diversion programmes agreed with undertakers
- [ ] Openreach / telecom protection plan in place
- [ ] Licensed contractor confirmed for utility works
- [ ] Temporary supplies arranged

## Site and neighbours

- [ ] Site boundary and access routes confirmed
- [ ] Neighbour liaison log initiated
- [ ] Noise/dust/hours restrictions per planning condition
- [ ] Archaeology / ecology constraints cleared if applicable

## CDM and H&S

- [ ] F10 notification submitted (if notifiable)
- [ ] Principal Designer and Principal Contractor appointed
- [ ] Pre-construction information pack assembled
- [ ] Construction phase plan received from Principal Contractor

## Programme hooks

- [ ] Mobilisation to main works sequence defined (`uk-construction-programme`)
- [ ] PCI pack index issued to contractor
- [ ] Utility diversion completion dates in master programme

**Halt:** Do not commence main works without valid BC approval and commencement conditions.
