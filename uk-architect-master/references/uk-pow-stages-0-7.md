# UK RIBA Plan of Work — Stages 0–7 checklists

Per-stage inputs, outputs, and gate criteria. Align with BSA gateways for HRB projects.

**Jurisdiction:** England default. Confirm Wales, Scotland, Northern Ireland for devolved Building Regulations.

---

## Stage 0 — Strategic definition

**Inputs:** Client brief, site constraints, policy context, budget envelope.

**Key tasks:**
- [ ] Confirm HRB classification (residential ≥18 m or ≥7 storeys)
- [ ] Feasibility options and order-of-cost study
- [ ] Procurement strategy outline (`uk-procurement-strategy`)
- [ ] Risk register initiated
- [ ] Gateway 1 planning (HRB) — if applicable at this stage

**Gate:** Strategic brief approved; proceed/no-proceed decision.

---

## Stage 1 — Preparation and briefing

**Inputs:** Approved strategic brief, stakeholder map.

**Key tasks:**
- [ ] Project brief and spatial requirements
- [ ] Consultant appointments and RACI (`uk-deliverables-workstages`)
- [ ] Planning pre-application if warranted
- [ ] CDM notifiability assessment (`uk-construction-health-safety`)
- [ ] Fee proposal and appointments (`uk-fee-proposal-strategy`)

**Gate:** Project brief signed off; team appointed.

---

## Stage 2 — Concept design

**Inputs:** Project brief, site survey, planning policy review.

**Key tasks:**
- [ ] Concept drawings and massing options
- [ ] Planning strategy and D&A Statement draft
- [ ] Fire strategy concept (`uk-fire-life-safety`)
- [ ] Sustainability targets Part L/O (`uk-building-sustainability`)
- [ ] Cost plan 1 (`uk-cost-consultancy`)

**Gate:** Concept approved; planning route confirmed.

---

## Stage 3 — Spatial coordination

**Inputs:** Approved concept, consultant inputs.

**Key tasks:**
- [ ] Coordinated Stage 3 package
- [ ] Planning submission if applicable (`uk-spatial-planning`)
- [ ] BR pre-application advice (`uk-building-codes`)
- [ ] Heritage / listed building consent if required
- [ ] Issue pack issued (`uk-deliverables-workstages`)

**Gate:** Coordinated design frozen for planning/technical progression.

---

## Stage 4 — Technical design

**Inputs:** Planning permission (or reserved matters), coordinated Stage 3.

**Key tasks:**
- [ ] Technical design drawings and NBS specification
- [ ] Full Plans Building Control submission
- [ ] HRB Gateway 2 application (if HRB)
- [ ] Tender documents and cost plan 3
- [ ] Procurement route confirmed (`uk-procurement-strategy`)

**Gate:** Tender issue or contractor appointment (per route).

---

## Stage 5 — Manufacturing and construction

**Inputs:** BC approval, contract in place, site establishment complete.

**Key tasks:**
- [ ] Site establishment (`uk-site-establishment`)
- [ ] IFC/issue-for-construction packages
- [ ] Site inspections and RFIs (`uk-site-supervision`)
- [ ] Gateway 2 hold-point evidence (HRB)
- [ ] Variations and valuations (`uk-tender-contract-administration`)
- [ ] Construction programme (`uk-construction-programme`)

**Gate:** Watertight / practical completion approach.

---

## Stage 6 — Handover

**Inputs:** Works substantially complete, commissioning underway.

**Key tasks:**
- [ ] BC completion certificate strategy (`uk-op-submission-strategy`)
- [ ] Gateway 3 evidence (HRB)
- [ ] Fire commissioning (`uk-fsd-licensing-compliance`)
- [ ] Regulation 38 pack (`uk-fire-life-safety`)
- [ ] Snagging and contractual PC (`uk-practical-completion-snagging`)
- [ ] s106 compliance (`uk-certificate-of-compliance`)

**Gate:** Lawful occupation and contractual PC achieved.

---

## Stage 7 — In use

**Inputs:** PC achieved, O&M manuals issued.

**Key tasks:**
- [ ] Post-occupancy evaluation brief
- [ ] Golden thread updates (HRB)
- [ ] Defects period administration
- [ ] Asset information updates (ISO 19650)

**Gate:** Final certificate and defects period close-out.
