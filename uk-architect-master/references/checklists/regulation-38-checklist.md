# Regulation 38 handover checklist (advisory)

Regulation 38 requires fire safety information to be given to the responsible person when a project is complete.

## Information themes

- [ ] Fire strategy report (final issue)
- [ ] Record drawings showing fire safety design (compartmentation, fire doors, routes)
- [ ] Specifications for fire-resisting construction and fire door sets
- [ ] Commissioning records for active measures (alarms, suppression, smoke control)
- [ ] Maintenance requirements and inspection regimes
- [ ] Contact details for fire strategy author and key suppliers

## Process

- [ ] Issue register with revision control
- [ ] Recipient (responsible person) identified
- [ ] Linked to practical completion and contract administrator records
