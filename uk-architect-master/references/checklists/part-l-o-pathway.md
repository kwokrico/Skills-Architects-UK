# Part L and Part O pathway (advisory)

## Part L (early design)

- [ ] Fabric performance targets agreed with envelope consultant
- [ ] SAP/SBEM modeller appointed for dwellings / non-domestic respectively
- [ ] TER/DER or BER/TER trajectory understood
- [ ] Renewables and LZC feasibility recorded

## Part O (early design)

- [ ] Glazing ratio and orientation risk screened
- [ ] Purge ventilation and shading strategy identified
- [ ] If screen = High: dynamic thermal modelling or TM59 route planned
- [ ] Coordination with Part F ventilation strategy
