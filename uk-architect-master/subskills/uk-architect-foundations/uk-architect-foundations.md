# UK Architect Foundations

For specialist statutory or technical depth, route via master `SKILL.md` to the relevant sub-skill.

## When to Use This Skill

| Question type | This skill | Use instead |
|---------------|------------|-------------|
| ARB Code, competence, conduct | `uk-architect-foundations` | — |
| RIBA stages and role clarity | `uk-architect-foundations` | topic-specific sub-skill |
| Building Regulations detail | `uk-architect-foundations` | `uk-building-codes` |
| Contract or PI liability | `uk-architect-foundations` | `uk-tender-contract-administration` / `uk-professional-indemnity` |

## Execution protocol

1. Frame answer against ARB Code of Conduct and RIBA professional standards (advisory).
2. Clarify architect appointment scope vs contractor / consultant duties.
3. Emphasise contemporaneous records, CPD, and delegated authority limits.
4. Route technical questions to appropriate sub-skill without duplicating specialist guidance.

## Outputs

- Practice governance note
- Scope and duty clarification
- Recommended specialist module(s) for follow-up

## Halt rules

- Halt on alleged misconduct or fitness to practise — recommend ARB processes and legal advice.
